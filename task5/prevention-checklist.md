# Prevention Checklist

## Prevention Tips for College Students:

Always verify the sender’s email address or message source before clicking on any link.

Avoid clicking on suspicious or shortened links received via SMS, email, or social media.

Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts and update them regularly.

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.

Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal information with anyone, even if they claim to be officials.

For UPI safety: Double-check the recipient’s name before sending money through UPI apps.

For UPI safety: Do not approve “collect requests” from unknown users or apps.

Regularly monitor your bank transactions and account activity for any unusual behavior.

Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.

Log out from shared or public devices after use.

## Report Cybercrime:

National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930

This checklist promotes responsible digital behavior and helps students stay alert and protected in an increasingly connected world.
