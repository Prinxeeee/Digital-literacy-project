# Description 
For this project, I used Canva to design my Digital Literacy Awareness poster. Canva helped me create a clean and organized layout while allowing me to include simple icons and balanced colors that make the information visually engaging. The main goal of my design is to raise awareness about important digital skills needed for learning, working, and communicating online. In the poster, I included a clear definition of digital literacy along with examples of useful digital tools such as note-taking apps, coding platforms, and communication tools. I also added a section on safe internet practices, highlighting the importance of using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal information. Additionally, I included an email etiquette section to promote respectful and professional online communication.

One thing I found interesting during this project was how design elements like spacing, icons, and color choices can make information easier to understand. At the same time, I found it challenging to include enough details without making the poster look crowded. Overall, this project helped me improve both my understanding of digital literacy and my design skills.
