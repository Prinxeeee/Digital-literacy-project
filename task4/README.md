## Task 4 – Email Etiquette

This folder contains two carefully composed professional emails along with a comprehensive social media do’s and don’ts checklist. Together, these elements demonstrate my understanding of effective and responsible digital communication.

The emails are structured to reflect proper email etiquette, including clear and relevant subject lines, respectful greetings, well-organized body paragraphs, and appropriate professional sign-offs. Each email maintains a formal tone, uses concise yet meaningful language, and avoids informal expressions or slang. This highlights my ability to communicate clearly and respectfully in both academic and professional contexts, whether requesting assistance or expressing interest in an opportunity.

In addition, the social media checklist outlines key guidelines for maintaining a positive and responsible online presence. It emphasizes important practices such as thinking before posting, protecting personal information, respecting others, and avoiding the spread of misinformation. It also includes reminders about appropriate language, digital footprints, and the impact of online behavior.

Overall, this task reflects my ability to apply digital etiquette principles across different platforms, ensuring communication that is professional, respectful, and mindful in today’s digital environment.
