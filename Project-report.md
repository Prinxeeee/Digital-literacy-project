## Task 4

A situation where poor digital communication caused a problem could be when a student misunderstood assignment instructions shared in a group chat. Due to unclear wording and lack of proper explanation, many students completed the task incorrectly, leading to confusion and loss of marks.

This problem could have been avoided if the message was clear, detailed, and properly structured. The sender should have used simple language and provided examples if necessary. Also, students should have asked questions if they were unsure instead of making assumptions.

This example shows that effective digital communication is very important. Clear messages, proper formatting, and timely clarification can prevent misunderstandings and ensure better outcomes.
